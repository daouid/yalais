Minkowski-Carathéodory Explorer
Visualizing Carathéodory's Theorem: Any point $x$ inside a compact convex set in $\mathbb{R}^d$ can be written as a convex combination of at most $d+1$ extreme points.
Dimension ($d$):2
Required extreme points ($d+1$):3
Total vertices:6
Convex hull vertices (Extreme points):5
Point location: Inside Hull
Drag the green target point inside the set. Drag any gray vertex to change the geometry of the set. Note how exactly 3 red extreme vertices are highlighted to represent the green point.
