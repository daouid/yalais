Mathematical Context

For a real algebraic integer β = ζd + ζd-1 in the cyclotomic field ℚ(ζd), its Galois conjugates are βj = 2·cos(2πj / d) for gcd(j, d) = 1.

The **house** is the maximum absolute value of these conjugates. The theorem proves that if the house is ≤ 2, it is either exactly 2 or matches 2·cos(π/m) for a positive integer m.
