Double Commutant Explorer
Visualizing the von Neumann Double Commutant Theorem: For a star-subalgebra $S$, its double commutant $S''$ is exactly its closure in the Strong Operator Topology (SOT) and Weak Operator Topology (WOT).

Drag the target operator T (blue dot) in the operator space. Drag the vector x (green arrow) in the Hilbert space. Observe how for any $T \in S''$ we can find $A \in S$ (yellow dot) that approximates $T$ on $x$ ($A x \approx T x$).
